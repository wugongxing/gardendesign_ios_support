‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

How does the AI garden design work?
Simply take a photo of your yard or outdoor space using your phone camera. Our AI analyzes the space and generates multiple landscape design options tailored to your location's climate and growing conditions.
Is my photo data secure?
Yes! Your photos are processed temporarily on our servers solely for generating designs. All images are automatically deleted immediately after processing - we don't store or keep copies of your photos.
Do I need to create an account?
No account required! The app works without usernames, passwords, or personal information. Just download and start designing your garden right away.
How are plant recommendations chosen?
Our AI uses your approximate location (city-level only) to suggest plants that thrive in your local climate zone, ensuring your garden design is both beautiful and sustainable.
What about the Amazon integration?
When accessing daily free credits, you may be redirected to Amazon's gardening section for product inspiration. This doesn't require any login and doesn't increase your purchase costs. It's simply a helpful resource for garden supplies.
